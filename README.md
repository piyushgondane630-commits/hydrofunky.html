HydroFunky is a fun, free water tracking app built entirely with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no downloads, no subscriptions, just open it in your browser and start hydrating.
The app lets you log water intake from 9 different containers — shot glass, regular glass, mug, 500ml bottle, 750ml bottle, 1L bottle, 2L bottle, sipper, and jug — each with its own colour-coded icon. Every container logs the exact millilitres for that vessel, so your tracking is always accurate no matter what you're drinking from.
At the heart of the app is an animated bottle that fills up with a wave effect as you log drinks, showing your percentage progress right on the bottle. A progress bar, daily ml counter, and streak tracker keep you motivated throughout the day.
What makes HydroFunky stand out is its built-in AI hydration coach, powered by Claude. It gives you personalised tips and motivational messages based on your name, your chosen container, and exactly how much you've drunk so far.
You can set a custom daily goal in millilitres, schedule reminders at your preferred interval, and reset your count each day. It works perfectly on mobile, tablet, and desktop — completely free, forever.
